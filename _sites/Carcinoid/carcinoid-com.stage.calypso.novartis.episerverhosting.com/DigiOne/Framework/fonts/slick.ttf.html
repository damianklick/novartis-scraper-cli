Welcome to Carcinoid.com

If you or a loved one has been diagnosed with carcinoid syndrome, a carcinoid tumor, or a neuroendocrine tumor (NET)—you’ve come to the right place. Each of these diagnoses begins with a rare type of tumor, called a NET, which arises from specialized hormone-releasing cells in the body.

Use this website to access useful information, support groups, and other resources for patients and caregivers.
